Why do you want to be a web developer?

I love the creative potential of coding and full stack development. It's more than just lines of code, it's being able to think abstractly, artistically, logically, and creatively all at the same time. It's learning a new language from one that I already know, and it's watching instant feedback stream across my screen that I know I did. I also love it because of the ability to see and change what's wrong almost instantaneously and jumping in to fix it before it causes too much damage. I get to flex a lot of different mental muscles that I haven't had the change to do before.
